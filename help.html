Frequently Ask Questions (FAQs)

What types of pet supplies do you offer?
Answer: Our store offers a wide range of pet supplies, including food and treats, grooming products, toys, bedding, and health supplements for various pets such as dogs, cats, birds, and small animals.
What is your return policy?
Answer: We accept returns within 30 days of purchase. The items must be unused, in their original packaging, and accompanied by the receipt. To initiate a return, please contact our customer service team, and they will provide you with further instructions.
Do you offer international shipping?
Answer: Currently, we only ship within the country. We are working on expanding our shipping options and hope to offer international shipping in the near future. Stay tuned for updates on our website and social media channels.
Can I change or cancel my order after it has been placed?
Answer: If you need to change or cancel your order, please contact our customer service team as soon as possible. If the order has not yet been processed or shipped, we will do our best to accommodate your request. However, once an order is shipped, changes or cancellations are not possible.
Are your pet foods and products safe and certified?
Answer: Yes, all our pet foods and products are sourced from reputable manufacturers and suppliers. They meet industry standards and are certified for safety and quality. We are committed to providing high-quality products that ensure the health and well-being of your pets.
